History of Janata Dal (Secular) – JDS

Formation (1999)

JDS was formed in 1999 after splitting from Janata Dal. It was founded by H. D. Deve Gowda.

Leadership

  • H. D. Deve Gowda – Founder
  • H. D. Kumaraswamy – Key leader

Political Growth

  • Strong presence in Karnataka
  • Support from farmers and rural population
  • 2006: Coalition with BJP
  • 2018: Coalition with Congress

Alliances

JDS often forms coalition governments and plays a kingmaker role.

Ideology

  • Secularism
  • Farmers welfare
  • Rural development
  • Regional focus
