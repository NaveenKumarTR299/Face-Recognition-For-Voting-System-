History of Shiv Sena (SS)

Formation (1966)

Shiv Sena was founded in 1966 by Bal Thackeray in Maharashtra.

Early Ideology

  • Marathi pride (sons of the soil)
  • Regional identity
  • Employment for locals

Political Growth

  • Strong base in Maharashtra
  • Alliance with BJP
  • 1995: First government in Maharashtra

Recent Developments

  • Leadership change after Bal Thackeray
  • Split into factions (Uddhav vs Shinde)

Ideology

  • Regional nationalism
  • Hindutva
  • Strong governance
