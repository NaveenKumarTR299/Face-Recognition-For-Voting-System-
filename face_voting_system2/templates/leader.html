Narendra Modi

Prime Minister of India

Narendra Modi is the current Prime Minister of India and a leader of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP). He has played a key role in shaping modern India’s policies and development.

Rahul Gandhi

Leader of Indian National Congress

Rahul Gandhi is a prominent leader of the Indian National Congress (INC). He has served as the President of the party and plays an important role in Indian politics, focusing on social justice, equality, and democratic values.

Mayawati

Leader of Bahujan Samaj Party

Mayawati is a prominent leader of the Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP) and a former Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh. She is known for her work towards social justice and empowerment of marginalized communities.

H.D. Kumaraswamy

Leader of Janata Dal (Secular)

H.D. Kumaraswamy is a senior leader of Janata Dal (Secular) and former Chief Minister of Karnataka. He is known for his focus on rural development and farmer welfare.

Uddhav Thackeray

Leader of Shiv Sena

Uddhav Thackeray is the leader of Shiv Sena and former Chief Minister of Maharashtra. He is known for his leadership in regional politics and development initiatives.
