History of Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP)

Formation (1984)

BSP was founded in 1984 by Kanshi Ram. The party focuses on empowering marginalized communities.

Leadership

  • Kanshi Ram – Founder
  • Mayawati – Key leader

Political Growth

  • Strong presence in Uttar Pradesh
  • Focus on Dalit and backward communities
  • 2007: Majority government in UP

Ideology

  • Social justice
  • Equality
  • Empowerment of marginalized communities
  • Anti-caste discrimination
